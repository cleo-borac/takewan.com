What we do

Digital electronic circuit design

Schematic capture

Capabilities

2-, 4-, or 6- layer boards

through-hole and SMD, including BGA

trace length matching (e.g. for SDR SDRAM)

We do not do designs requiring:

  • blind and/or buried vias
  • micro vias
  • back drilling
  • DDRx
  • EMC compliance

Pricing

Pricing is based on the complexity of the work. Hence, it is quoted based on the specific project. However, because our in-house tools are extremely efficient, we are able to quote you very attractive prices. Contact us today for a quote!

The price quoted includes one design, reasonable individualized assistance, and unlimited free redesigns if you are unable to make the prototype pass functionality tests.
